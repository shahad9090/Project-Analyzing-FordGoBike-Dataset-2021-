Introduction¶

In this project, we aim to analyze data Ford GoBike is a regional public bicycle sharing system in the San Francisco Bay Area, California. Beginning operation in August 2013 as Bay Area Bike Share, the Ford GoBike system currently has over 2,600 bicycles in 262 stations across San Francisco, East Bay and San Jose. On June 28, 2017, the system officially launched as Ford GoBike in a partnership with Ford Motor Company.

Ford GoBike It consists like other bicycle systems and is a system throughout the city, allowing users to move around and be able to use different stations around the city by opening the use of the bicycle from one station and the ability to retrieve it from another station, in order to reach the highest category from users.
